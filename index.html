Jia Li (李佳)

I am an incoming PhD student at Monash University, where I will be supervised by Prof. TT Wong and Prof. Jianfei Cai. I completed my Master's Degree in June 2025 at Shandong University, majoring in Software Engineering, under the supervision of Prof. Lu Wang and Prof. Beibei Wang.

My research interests primarily focus on computer graphics and 3D vision, leveraging emerging technologies to explore better 3D representation, reconstruction and generation.

I was very fortunate to spend 4 months (March 2024 - July 2024) at Tencent IEG MoreFun Studio and summer of 2023 at NanKai University with Prof. Beibei Wang.
